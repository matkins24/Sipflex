Teams Integration Benefits

SIP & Teams Twinning

We empower you to operate your VoIP phones concurrently with Microsoft Teams, allowing calls to ring on both Teams and your SIP phones simultaneously. This provides the flexibility to use either Teams or your existing handsets for calls, ensuring peace of mind in case of any issues with your Teams client.

Automated Failover

Choose to automatically reroute calls to an alternative phone number if your Teams client becomes unavailable. You can direct calls to a VoIP phone or app, or forward them to your mobile or another fixed line on any network. This ensures confidence that no incoming calls will be missed during Teams client problems.

Create Hybrid Solutions

Mix line types, with some users utilizing Microsoft Teams and others using regular VOIP handsets. Reduce Microsoft licensing costs by purchasing licenses only for users requiring Teams. The blend of Teams and Tel2-only users can form a unified calling group, facilitating seamless call transfers between all users.

Cloud Integration

Enhance your Microsoft Teams phone service by leveraging advanced calling features in Tel2's Cloud PBX, reducing additional Microsoft license costs. Features include Call Recording, Simultaneous Ring Groups, Auto Attendants, Hunt Groups, Call Screening, Call Parking, PIN Codes, and our Fax to Email service.

Teams Capable Handsets

Tel2 supports "Teams Capable" handsets from Yealink, such as the T55A, T56A, and T58A desk phones. These phones seamlessly integrate with your Microsoft Teams environment, providing Twinning functionality where they can function as both a Teams client and a regular VoIP handset simultaneously. Self manage these phones in our web portal.

Great Value and No Contracts

Unlike most other 'Teams' integration providers, Tel2 has no fixed-term contracts or minimum user requirements. You can choose to migrate any number of users to the Hero Teams service without minimum spend or contracts, allowing cancellation at any time.
