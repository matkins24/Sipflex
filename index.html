How Sipflex Works

Sipflex makes building a robust communications plan easy with our flexible SIP offerings

  • 01 Start a free trial

    Sign up for a free trial. Choose any local UK phone number to use for the trial and enjoy 60 minutes of free UK calling. No credit card is required for the trial.

    Follow our configuration guides to configure your PBX of choice. Test our service to ensure that Sipflex will suit your needs.

What are non-geographic numbers?

A non-geographic (030) number is a telephone number that is not tied to a specific geographic location. They provide a single point of contact for customers or clients regardless of their location.

Calls to 03 numbers cost the same as calls to geographic numbers (starting with 01 or 02) and are included in inclusive minutes on landlines and mobiles.
